Hi Everybody!

I'm Fair Flower Mejor Rees and this is my Family.

I created this blog to bring back families together. Processing a Spouse Visa seems to be a tedious and difficult process to go throught, and I have to be honest it was the most stressful time of our lives. A lot of argument and a lot of sleepless nights, distance took its toll and talking to each other on a phone camera does make it a little bit too hard to communicate effectively. My husband and I met in the Maldives where we both work, we lived on the same Island so he was quite familiar to me. I am quite introvert and rather keep myself to myself, I do have favourite people to be with but it takes time for me to warm up to someone. Maybe because I know that I try too hard to be liked that I do awkward stuff and end up having no friends because they think that I am obnoxious or something.
