Let's sort out your documents

"Gathering documets for your visa is the most tedious part of the application. But,... it doesn't have to be"

Those documents you need?... They are just within your reach and easy to find! All you need to make sure is to only compile the Documents you need nothing else.

Let's Start with the easy one's!
  • Proof of Identification:
    (Apart from yours and your partner/husband/wife (sponsor's) "PASSPORT" You will need to include another Government issued ID)

    Such As:

    • Driver's License
    • National ID

      I know that it's incredibly impossibe to get Philippine Issued ID's, so here are your options:

      • UMID ID
        click here to find out more
      • NBI Clearance
        click here to find out more
      • Philpost ID
        click here to find out more

      Only send a back to back copy of either one of them

  • Proof of Address:

    Proof of where you are staying in the UK

    Such As:

    • Mortgage Letter
    • Letter from Landlord
    • Caouncil Tax
    • Utility Bill
  • Proof of Relationship:

    Marriage Contract is the best way to prove your relationship there is no need for other documentation but you are welcome to send compiled photos but this is absolutly not necessary

  • Proof of Finances:

    You need to provide both

    1. 6 Months Payslip
    2. 6 Months Bank Statement
